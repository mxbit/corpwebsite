We designed SCUBI Command monitoring LED panel electronics circuit schematic to meet functional requirements, created Gerber file based on ISRO-PAX-304, fabricated of multilayer PCBs, carried out quality control verification of the PCBs, soldered the components including SMD devices, tested the functionality according to product specification and supplied this finished electronic package meeting the specifications of ISRO.

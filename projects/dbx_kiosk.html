Kiosk Photobooth

Touch Screen Kiosk for sending e-Cards for socially connected tourists coming in Dubai International Airports. Kiosk app is a brand building programme for brand Dubai Airports and Dubai Tourism. User can take photos and create constom e-cards with their photos imposed on it.

MXBIT built this to for Papercritics for the brand promition exercise of Dubai Aiports, by collecting tourist informations. The app collects the log of tourists uses the kiosk. Built on Adobe Air and SQlite.

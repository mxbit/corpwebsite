Aabar Properties LLC - Real estate Project App

Aabar Properties LLC

Aabar Properties LLC is a real estate development company, participated Cityscape 2012 Abu Dhabi, they wanted to showcase all their current project in an iPad.

MXBIT created an HTML5 iPad app with 10 projects including their floor plans, master plan and photo gallery, location map both in English and Arabic. The app has an intuitive UI and easy navigation using the potentials of HTML5, CSS3 and jQuery and used PhoneGap to compile the iPad app.
